Ryan Boal is a healthcare technology, analytics, and AI leader who helps organizations turn complex data into practical solutions that drive measurable business improvement.

Over a career spanning software engineering, healthcare product development, and management consulting, Ryan has delivered platforms, reporting systems, and decision-support capabilities for some of the most sophisticated players in healthcare — including health plans, pharmacy benefit managers, and pharmacy organizations operating at national scale.

What Ryan does best is bridge the gap between strategy and execution.

He understands what works at the highest levels — and knows how to implement it in the real world.

Today, Ryan focuses on helping organizations apply modern analytics and AI in ways that are actionable, scalable, and outcomes-driven — without the delays, politics, or inertia that often slow transformation inside large enterprises.

He brings proven industry best practices and “right-sizes” them for smaller and mid-sized organizations that want the same advantages as the big players — without reinventing the wheel or learning through costly trial and error.

Ryan’s applied expertise includes:

• Advanced analytics and performance reporting
• Dashboarding and business visibility (Power BI, Tableau)
• SQL- and Python-based automation and insight generation
• Cloud data pipelines and modern data architecture
• Machine learning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and agent-enabled workflows
• Responsible AI systems that enhance — not replace — human decision-making

Ryan is passionate about helping teams move from AI curiosity to AI-enabled execution — delivering solutions that improve productivity, accelerate decision-making, and provide clear, measurable value over time.

With the mindset of a builder and the perspective of a strategist, Ryan helps organizations learn from what the industry leaders have already proven — and apply it faster, smarter, and more effectively..
